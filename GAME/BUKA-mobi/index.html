Game guide

Welcome to Moomin Valley

You are Buka – a frigid force. Eliminate all Moomins while dealing with Snufkin and Hattifatteners.

Controls

  • Move: WASD or Arrows.
  • Bolt (ice shot): Ctrl – consumes bolt ammo, freezes the target.
  • Frost cloud: hold Shift to charge:
    • Tap: small cloud (7s), freezes nearby.
    • ≥3s: strong cloud (bigger radius, 30s freeze), costs 10 clouds.
    • ≥6s: global freeze of the whole board for 60s, costs 100 clouds.
  • P – pause/resume.
  • R – restart level.

Goal

Eliminate all Moomins on each level. The Progress bar shows completion. There are 30 levels with rising difficulty and themes.

Resources & HUD

  • HP: green bar – avoid Snufkin’s flames and contact with Hattifatteners.
  • Clouds: blue bar – used for clouds and global freeze.
  • Bolts: light blue bar – each shot costs 1.
  • Global Freeze: time remaining if active.
  • Level code: 7 chars – can be used on start screen to jump to a level.

Objects

  • Moomins: white beings – when they see you, they run to the house. Outside the house you can extinguish them by proximity.
  • Snufkin (Hunter): greenish – shoots flames (damage over time). Freeze with bolts/clouds. Periodically calls Hattifatteners; after many freezes, he disappears.
  • Hattifatteners: yellowish sparks – chase Buka; contact deals heavy damage, then they flee. Bolt hits can make them grow.
  • Bobek: helper – call him in the cave (stand in the center and release Shift). Builds webs (traps); Snufkin can burn them.
  • Traps (webs): immobilize Moomins; burn away over time.
  • Bolt pickup: blue spark – +10 bolt ammo.
  • Cloud pickup: pale cloud – +5 clouds.
  • Mushrooms: red caps – “growth” for 15s (3x speed, bigger reach), then fades.
  • House: 3×3 passable – Moomins hide there; can’t be eliminated inside.
  • Cave: corner of the map – Bobek call point.

Tactics

  • Combine clouds and bolts – clouds limit movement, bolts freeze threats.
  • Place webs on house–corridor routes to catch fleeing Moomins.
  • Watch Snufkin’s aim and freeze him opportunistically.
  • Save Global Freeze for crises – it’s expensive.
  • Use mushrooms to sweep tough sections.

Level codes

Every level has a deterministic 7-char code. Enter it on the start screen and press “Codes” to start at that level (mechanics unchanged).

Music

“Music ON/OFF” toggles the background. During panic (Hattifatteners) a different track plays.

Good luck!

Experiment with map, weather and themes. Each level is harder and more interesting. Good luck, Buka!
